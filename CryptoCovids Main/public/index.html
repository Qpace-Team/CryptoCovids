The Games general idea :

The CryptoCovids are completely practical and special, and for a specific and purposeful purpose, 20210 of them exist. Each CryptoCovid is considered a token that is supposed to be used in a special and unique game. Since for fear of Copying the idea, we can't unravel the details of the game at this time, we have come with just generals. A Weekly Pseudo-gambling game has been designed which is exclusively for the CryptoCovids Community, that after minting all 20210 CryptoCovids and surely with the previous announcement, will be activated. Each of the CryptoCovids as a token will have permission for entry to the game, and they will level up or down based on their last price and their chance to win will increase or decrease based on it. Finally, the game will have 210 winners weekly, and the prizes will be split between them in a specific order.
